Axioms of Probability  
* Non-negativity:
  * for any event $E \in F, p(E) \geq 0$
* All possible outcomes:
  * p($\Omega$)=1
* Additivity of disjoint events:
  * For all events $E, E' \in F$ where $E \cap E' = \emptyset$,  $p(E \cup E') = p(E)+p(E')$
